VocabuBaby Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 25, 2024

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use VocabuBaby:

  • Account Information – When you create an account via Firebase Authentication, we collect your email address only.
  • Usage and Analytics Data – Firebase Analytics collects device information, usage patterns, and country-level location data. This information is collected anonymously and cannot be used to identify individual users.
  • Crash and Performance Data – We collect crash reports and performance metrics to help us identify and fix technical issues. No personally identifiable information (PII) is included in this data.

Information We Do NOT Collect

VocabuBaby does not collect:

  • Personal information from children
  • Photos or images
  • Precise location data
  • Payment information
  • Content viewed or created within the app

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Maintain and manage your account
  • Improve the app's features and user experience
  • Identify and fix bugs and technical issues
  • Understand how users interact with VocabuBaby to guide future development

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored and processed using Firebase Authentication and Firebase Analytics, both services provided by Google. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. While no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data.

Third-Party Services

VocabuBaby uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication – Used to manage user accounts securely.
  • Firebase Analytics – Used to collect anonymous usage data to improve the app.

Both services are provided by Google. For more information on how Google handles data, please review the Google Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

VocabuBaby is designed for use by parents and caregivers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The app requires a parent or caregiver to create an account in order to use VocabuBaby. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information without parental consent, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate action.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access – Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Delete – Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt Out of Analytics – Opt out of anonymous analytics collection through your device settings.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at vocabubabyapp@gmail.com.

Data Retention

Your account information is retained for as long as your account remains active. If you choose to delete your account, your personal data will be removed from our systems. Anonymous analytics data may be retained beyond account deletion as it cannot be linked to any individual user and is used solely to improve the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page along with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
